* 10.06.83 Retired FunFlow use-module Macro IR

This document path is retained only as a stable Org link target.  The former
=use-module= public syntax and Macro IR contract are removed.  They are not a
compatibility surface and MUST NOT be used by new code, documentation, tests,
or generated Agent guidance.

The canonical architecture and vocabulary are defined by
[[file:136-gerbil-module-feature-layering.org][RFC 136 — Gerbil Module And POO Flow Feature Layering]].  FunFlow is selected
as a POO-native =Feature= through =use-feature=.  Gerbil =module=, =import=, and
=export= remain language-level compilation and namespace mechanisms; they do
not represent product feature activation.
