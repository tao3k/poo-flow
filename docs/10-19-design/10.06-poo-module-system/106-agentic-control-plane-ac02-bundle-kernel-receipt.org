#+title: AC-02 Immutable Bundle Kernel Qualification Receipt

* Result

AC-02 adds a POO-native, pure functional lifecycle kernel over the AC-01
Canonical Organization Bundle.  It provides immutable candidate, validated,
and advanced states plus deterministic open, validate, no-op, conflict, stale,
rejection, and advance receipts.

* Qualification

The AC-02 scenario passes six cases and nineteen checks covering open/validate,
successful advance, previous-state immutability, no-op stability, identity and
epoch conflicts, invalid next Bundle evidence, and invalid-phase rejection.

AC-01's eight cases and twenty checks remain the semantic regression gate.
AC-02 does not introduce persistence, locks, C handles, ABI layout, Python,
Cedar, Lean, or downstream runtime behavior.
