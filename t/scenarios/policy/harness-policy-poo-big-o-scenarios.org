#+TITLE: Harness Policy POO Big-O Scenarios

* Purpose

POO performance benchmarks must be real downstream scenarios, not standalone
metric declarations. Every Big-O family scenario added for POO Flow must carry
both sides of the repair:

- input: the downstream shape before optimization.
- expected: the POO-native optimized shape after repair.

* Scenario Families

- t/scenarios/performance/poo-runtime-request-family
- t/scenarios/performance/poo-policy-proof-fact-family
- t/scenarios/performance/poo-strategy-profile-family
- t/scenarios/performance/poo-durable-receipt-family
- t/scenarios/performance/poo-benchmark-fixture-family
- t/scenarios/performance/poo-marlin-runtime-handoff-profile

* Policy

- A benchmark-only fixture is incomplete.
- A Big-O scenario must include benchmark.ss, input/src, and expected/src.
- The benchmark fixture must point at the input and expected repair files with
  inputPath and expectedPath.
- Unsafe same-name POO slot/value forms remain in the bounded leak scenario
  only; optimized expected code must use renamed lexical values.
